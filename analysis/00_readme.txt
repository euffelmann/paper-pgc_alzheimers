analysis/
=========

Output directory for all analyses in the AD GWAS meta-analysis project
(PGC-ALZ3). Each subdirectory corresponds to a distinct analysis step.
Scripts that generate the contents of each subdirectory are listed under
the relevant entry; all scripts live in the project root under analysis/.


Subdirectories
--------------

eQTL_coloc/
  Colocalization analysis between AD GWAS signals and eQTL data.

figures/
  All output figures for the manuscript and supplementary materials.
  Subdirectories mirror the analysis that produced each figure.
  Figures are generated by the following scripts:
    - eadb_vs_main.R      p-value scatter: EADB vs. PGC-ALZ3
    - h2l_curve.R         h2l as a function of disease prevalence
    - h2l_r2l_bar.R       h2l (LDSC vs. SBayesRC) and incremental R2l bar plots
    - manhattan.R         Manhattan plots for all ancestry × stratum combinations
    - pgs_r2l.R           Incremental PGS R2l bar plots (SBayesRC and SBayesRC-mult)
    - repro.R             Beta correlation scatter plots (EADB and PGC-ALZ2 vs. PGC-ALZ3)
    - sex_miami.R         Miami plots comparing female vs. male meta-analysis results

flames/
  FLAMES gene prioritisation scores for genome-wide significant loci.
  Used to annotate index variants with gene names in manhattan.R and
  sex_miami.R.

lava/
  Local Analysis of [co]Variant Association (LAVA) results estimating
  local SNP heritability and genetic correlations between AD and other
  complex traits across genome-wide significant AD loci.
  Generated by:
    - lava.R              Prepares sumstats, writes LAVA input files,
                          submits jobs, and collects results.

ldsc/
  LD Score Regression results, including SNP heritability (h2),
  genetic correlations (rg), and munged summary statistics.
  Generated by:
    - ldsc_munge.R        Prepares and munges sumstats for LDSC input.
                          Handles per-cohort, meta-analysis, and
                          other-trait sumstats. Submits jobs via
                          src/ldscMunge.sh.
    - ldsc_h2.R           Submits LDSC h2 jobs and collects results
                          into analysis/ldsc/ldsc_h2.txt. Includes a
                          sensitivity analysis with --chisq-max 999999.
                          Submits jobs via src/ldscH2.sh.
    - ldsc_rg.R           Submits LDSC rg jobs (proxy vs. case-control,
                          female vs. male, AD vs. other traits) and
                          collects results into analysis/ldsc/ldsc_rg/.
                          Submits jobs via src/ldscRg.sh.

metasoft/
  METASOFT/RE2 heterogeneity analysis outputs for the meta-analysis.

pgs/
  Polygenic score evaluation results across cohorts and ancestry groups.
  Generated by:
    - pgs_eval.R          Computes R2 on the liability scale and AUC
                          for SBayesRC EUR PGS and multi-ancestry
                          SBayesRC-mult PGS across all evaluation
                          cohorts. Saves results to
                          analysis/pgs/pgs_eval.rds.

risk_loci/
  Genome-wide significant locus definitions (index variants, windows,
  gene annotations) for all meta-analysis strata. Used by manhattan.R,
  sex_miami.R, repro.R, and lava.R.
  Risk loci are identified using riskloc() (R/riskloc.R), which clusters
  significant variants into independent loci using ±250 kb windows.
